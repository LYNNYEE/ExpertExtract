          <h2>在其他部门/单位工作仍在理学院招生的教师名录</h2>
          <div class="box_list">
            <h3>张普敦</h3>
                                                                            <div class="zuozhe">  		      时间：2015-04-08    阅读次数：<script src="/cms/web/count.jsp?articleID=56914"></script></div>
            <div class="wz_art">
              <p><span style="font-size: 16px">男；副教授、硕士生导师，群众<img alt="" onclick="window.open('../../../images/content/2017-09/20170911162915425161_1.jpg')" src="../../../images/content/2017-09/20170911162915425161.jpg" style="border-bottom: 2px solid; border-left: 2px solid; margin: 10px 2px; width: 300px; float: right; height: 275px; border-top: 2px solid; border-right: 2px solid"><br>
电话：010－64417634<br>
电子邮箱：<a href="mailto:zhangpd@mail.buct.edu.cn" target="_blank">zhangpd@mail.buct.edu.cn</a><br>
实验室楼名及房间号：综合楼224,226</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 16px"> 目前主讲课程：<br>
本科生：分析化学，化学实验设计<br>
研究生：近代仪器分析（光谱部分）</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 16px"> 教育经历：<br>
1991年毕业于成都科技大学（现与四川大学合并）化学系，获理学学士学位（分析化学）；<br>
1998年毕业于兰州大学化学系，获理学硕士学位（分析化学）；<br>
2003年毕业于中国科学院大连化学物理研究所，获理学博士学位（分析化学）。<br>
&nbsp;<br>
 工作经历：<br>
1991.07－1999.08：中石油研究院兰州中心物化分析室，工程师；<br>
2003.03－2003.06：中国科学院大连化学物理研究所生物技术部，研究人员；<br>
2003.07－2005.07：上海交通大学化学化工学院/环境科学与工程学院，博士后；<br>
2005.08－至今：&nbsp;&nbsp; 北京化工大学理学院/分析测试中心，副教授；<br>
2014.08－2015.08：德国杜伊斯堡-埃森大学（Univ. Duisburg-Essen）物理化学系，访问学者。<br>
&nbsp;<br>
 学术兼职：<br>
（1）美国应用光谱学会（Society of Applied Spectroscopy）会员，中国化学会会员；<br>
（2）“Vibrational Spectroscopy”、“Microchimica Acta”、“RSC Advances”、“Journal of Molecular Structure”、“Analytical Methods”、“Spectroscopy Letters”、“Langmuir”、“I &amp; EC”以及“中国科学-化学”、“科学通报”、“分析化学”、 “大学化学”、“天然产物研究及应用”等多个学术期刊的审稿人。<br>
&nbsp;<br>
 主要研究领域：<br>
（1）各种分子振动光谱（如红外、近红外、拉曼等）原理、技术升级及应用；<br>
（2）分子光谱成像（红外成像、拉曼成像）、数据分析以及应用；<br>
（3）二维相关光谱（2DCOS）；<br>
（4）表面增强光谱技术（SEIRAS、SERS）；<br>
（5）红外光谱技术在生物、材料领域的应用研究。</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 16px"> 主持或参与课题及项目研究情况：<br>
（1）主持：中国博士后科学基金项目，防化研究院、中石化润滑油研究院以及各类军工、民用科研项目十余项；<br>
（2）参与：国家自然科学基金及重大研究计划两项。</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 16px"> 发表论文情况：<br>
累计发表学术论文40余篇，其中SCI收录论文26篇，论文SCI他引200余次，单篇最高引用61次。近年来的代表性论文如下：<br>
(1) 张普敦著，第八章 红外光谱新技术，见：柯以侃，董慧茹编，分析化学手册第三分册（光谱分册）（第三版），化学工业出版社，pp409-511，2016年10月出版。<br>
(2)&nbsp; Zhiwei Yan, Qian Li, and Pudun Zhang*, Soy Protein Isolate and Glycerol Hydrogen Bonding Using Two-Dimensional Correlation (2D-COS) Attenuated Total Reflection Fourier Transform Infrared (ATR FT-IR) Spectroscopy, Applied Spectroscopy, 2017, in press, DOI: 10.1177/0003702817710249.<br>
(3)&nbsp; Qian Li, Yongjiao Tang, Zhiwei Yan, Pudun Zhang*, Identification of trace additives in polymer materials by attenuated total reflection Fourier transforminfrared mapping coupled with multivariate curve resolution, Spectrochimica Acta Part A: Molecular and Biomolecular Spectroscopy, 2017, 180, 154-160.<br>
(4) Pudun Zhang*, Miriam Unger, Frank Pfeifer, Heinz W. Siesler, Variable-temperature Fourier-transform infrared studies of poly(L-lactic acid) in different states of order: A 2DCOS and PCMW2D analysis, Journal of Molecular Structure, 2016, 1124, 256-261.<br>
(5)&nbsp;Yongjiao Tang, Nan Jing, Pudun Zhang*, Attenuated total reflectance/Fourier transform infrared (ATR/FTIR) mapping coupled with multivariate curve resolution (MCR) for studying the miscibility of chlorobutyl rubber/polyamide-12 blends, Applied Spectroscopy, 2015, 69 (11), 1319-1326.<br>
(6)&nbsp;Nan Jing, Xiaoting Jiang, Qian Wang, Yongjiao Tang, Pudun Zhang*, Attenuated total reflectance/Fourier transform infrared (ATR/FTIR) mapping coupled with principal component analysis for the study of in vitro degradation of porous polylactide/hydroxyapatite composite material, Analytical Methods, 2014, 6 (15), 5590-5595.<br>
(7)&nbsp;Yanying Zhang, Yangguang Li, Pudun Zhang*, De novo growth of poly(amidoamine) dendrimers on the surface of multi-walled carbon nanotubes, Journal of Materials Science, 2014, 49 (9), 3469-3477.<br>
(8)&nbsp;Wang Qian, Jiang Xiao-Ting, Xin Yun-Zi, Cui Jun-Xian, Zhang Pu-Dun*, Characterization of In vitro Mineralization of Porous Poly (L-Lactic Acid)/Bioactive Glass Composites by ATR-FTIR Mapping, Chinese Journal of Analytical Chemistry, 2014, 42(2),221-226.（优先出版）<br>
(9)&nbsp;Yanming Zhou, Bobin Li, Pudun Zhang*, Fourier Transform Infrared (FT-IR) Imaging Coupled withPrincipal Component Analysis (PCA) for the Study of Photooxidation of Polypropylene, Applied Spectroscopy, 2012, 66(5), 566-573.<br>
(10)&nbsp;LI BoBin, ZHANG PuDun*, Depth profiling of SBS/PET layered materials using step-scan phase modulation Fourier transform infrared photoacoustic spectroscopy and two-dimensional correlation analysis, Science China-Chemistry,2010, 53(5), 1190-1194.<br>
(11)&nbsp;Xin Zhou, Pudun Zhang*, Xiaoting Jiang, Guoying Rao, Influence of maleic anhydride grafted polypropylene on the miscibility of polypropylene/polyamide-6 blends Using ATR-FTIR mapping, Vibrational Spectroscopy, 2009, 49(1), 17-21.<br>
(12)&nbsp;Pudun Zhang*, Jiasui He, Xin Zhou, An FTIR standard addition method for quantification of bound styrene in its copolymers, Polymer Testing, 2008, 27(2), 153-157.<br>
(13)&nbsp;Xin Zhou, Pudun Zhang*, Zhifa Li, Guoying Rao, Miscibility behavior of ethylene/vinyl acetate and C5 petroleum resin by FTIR imaging, Analytical Science, 2007, 23(7), 877-880.<br>
&nbsp;<br>
 招生专业：<br>
分析化学、应用化学。<br>
&nbsp;<br>
 招生要求：<br>
勤奋，独立，正派，合作，善于思考，有较强动手能力，四级及以上英语水平。</span></p>
