        <div class="rt_tit">
        <h1>许梦卿</h1>
                    </div>
        <div class="rt_zuozhe">
      来源：文法学院&nbsp;&nbsp;发布日期：2013-12-13
        </div>
        <div class="rt_zhengwen" style="min-height:300px;_height:300px; height:auto;">
			&nbsp;
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt"><img alt="" src="../../../../images/content/2013-12/20131213142440656048.jpg" width="200" height="150" align="left"><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 许梦卿，生于<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，应用语言学硕士，现任北京化工大学文法学院副教授。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt"><br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
一、<b>教育背景及工作经历：</b></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1994</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月于东北师范大学外语系获文学学士学位，</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2008</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月于北京师范大学获应用语言学硕士学位，</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2012</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月赴<span style="background: white; letter-spacing: -0.75pt; color: black">美国宾西法尼<span class="grame">亚州</span>立大学应用语言学系访问研究。</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; background: white; letter-spacing: -0.75pt; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1994</span><span style="line-height: 150%; background: white; letter-spacing: -0.75pt; color: black; font-size: 12pt">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月起，任教于北京化工大学文法学院外语系，教授本科生大学英语课程。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; background: white; letter-spacing: -0.75pt; color: black; font-size: 12pt">二、</span><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">主要研究方向：</span></b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">英语语言教学。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">三、著作：</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 23.5pt"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">发表论文十余篇，其中“非英语专业大学生介词<span lang="EN-US">in</span>使用情况——一项基于语料库的研究”一文发表于外语类核心期刊《外语与外语教学》（<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年第<span lang="EN-US">5</span>期），“基于语料库的介词<span lang="EN-US">in</span>错误分析”发表于社科类核心期刊《宁夏大学学报（社会科学版）》（<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年第<span lang="EN-US">6</span>期），“<span lang="EN-US">Macroadaptaions of Textbooks for Particular Classroom Settings</span>”一文发表于世界英语教师协会<span lang="EN-US"> (TESOL) </span>出版的系列丛书 “<span lang="EN-US">The TESOL Classroom Series</span>”之<span lang="EN-US">Using Textbooks Effectively</span>（<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>）中。另参编教材五部。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">四、所获荣誉：</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2010</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月被评为我校优秀青年主讲教师。</span></div>
