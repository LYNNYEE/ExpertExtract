        <h1>刘小梅</h1>
                    </div>
        <div class="rt_zuozhe">
      来源：文法学院&nbsp;&nbsp;发布日期：2013-12-11
        </div>
        <div class="rt_zhengwen" style="min-height:300px;_height:300px; height:auto;">
			&nbsp;
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt"><img alt="" src="../../../../images/content/2013-12/20131211145035093762.jpg" width="200" height="257" align="left"><br>
<br>
刘小梅，女，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1960</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年生。民族：汉。籍贯：河北张家口市。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">职称：教授。学历：本科。学位：文学士。职务：北京化工大学语言教学实验中心主任。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt"><br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
一、</span></b><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">学习经历</span></b><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">:</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;</span>1978</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">——1982</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月在安徽大学外语系学习英语语言文学。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;</span>1994</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">9</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">—1996</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月在北京外国语大学进修研究生主要课程。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;</span>1997</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">9</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">—1998</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月在北京外国语大学参加北京高校外语教学高级研讨班。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;</span>2009</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">—7</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月，作为访问学者，在美国休斯顿大学英语系进修了当代语言学、英语语言史等课程。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">二、工作经历</span></b><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;:&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1982</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月在北京橡胶工业研究院从事翻译工作。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1985</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">7</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月至今，在北京化工大学外语部、文法学院外语系从事大学英语教学工作。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">三、</span><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">荣誉：</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">荣获</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1995—1996</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">学年度校级优秀青年主讲教师称号。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">荣获</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1998</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年北京市高等学校优秀青年骨干教师称号。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><b><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">四、科研：</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">（一）著作</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%; text-indent: 12pt" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">《征服基础英语语法》，独著。</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">43</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">万字。北京：北京大学出版社，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2005</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">（第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">次印刷）。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">《简明大学英汉对比语法》，独著。</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">60</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">万字。北京：北京化工出版社，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2008</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">《英汉语法对比研究》，独著。</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">23.7</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">万字。北京：北京化工出版社，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2011</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">（二）论文</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%; text-indent: 12pt" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp; </span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">提高非英语专业学生的英语交际能力之策略，第一作者，宁夏大学学报，约</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">6000</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">字，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2008</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">30</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">卷第二期。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%; text-indent: 24pt" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">论英汉<a name="OLE_LINK1">介词</a>与介词短语的异同，独立完成。《河北北方学院学报》，约</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">7000</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">字，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2008</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">年第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">24</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">卷第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">6</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">期。该文</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">被《语言文字学》杂志</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2009</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">04</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">期转载。（根据中国人民大学</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">“</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">复印报刊资料</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">”</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">转载统计）</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%; text-indent: 24pt" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">论英汉被动句、中动句、被动动词句，独立完成。《河北北方学院学报》，约</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">8000</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">字。</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2011</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">年第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">27</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">卷第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="line-height: 150%; color: black; font-size: 12pt">期。该文</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">被《语言文字学》杂志</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2011</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">第</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">06</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">期转载。（根据中国人民大学</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">“</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">复印报刊资料</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">”</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">转载统计）</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">（三）教改立项</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">主持了教育部大学英语改革的扩展项目：提高交际能力与交际能力评估，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2004</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">8</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">—2005</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">7</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月。该立项于</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2005</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">8</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月结题，取得可信数据。</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; line-height: 150%; text-indent: 24pt" align="left"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">参与并完成了教育部大学英语改革立项：实用性英语教学改革与实践，</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2004</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">8</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">—2005</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">7</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月。该立项于</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2005</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">年</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">8</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">月圆满结题。</span></div>
