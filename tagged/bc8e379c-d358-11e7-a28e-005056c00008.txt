        <h1>徐宗钰</h1>
                    </div>
        <div class="rt_zuozhe">
      来源：文法学院&nbsp;&nbsp;发布日期：2014-05-15
        </div>
        <div class="rt_zhengwen" style="min-height:300px;_height:300px; height:auto;">
			&nbsp;
<div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12pt"><img alt="" src="../../../../images/content/2014-05/20140515155609000728.jpg" width="267" height="374">徐宗钰，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1968</span><span style="font-size: 12pt">年出生，副教授，主要研究方向：</span><span style="font-size: 12pt">应用语言学、教学法</span></div>
<div class="MsoListParagraph" style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: -24pt; margin-left: 24pt"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>一、</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">学术经历</span></div>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">毕业于天津师范大学外语系，之后于北京大学研究生院读研究生班，曾在美国</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">University of Huston</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">作</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">访问学者。</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"> <br>
</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-size: 12pt">二、工作经历</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt; text-indent: 24pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">1993</span><span style="font-size: 12pt">年于北京化工大学外语系任教，现任外语系主任助理。</span><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"> <br>
</span><span style="font-size: 12pt">三、研究项目</span><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"> <br>
</span><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2011-2012<span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">北京化工大学教师互动发展团队建设<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>教育部人文社会科学重点研</span></p>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt; text-indent: 66pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt">究基地北京外国语大学<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>外语教育研究中心“中国外语教育基金”</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt; text-indent: 66pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt">专项课题<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>主持人</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2008-2010<span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">外语系网络互动语言教学研究与实践<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>校级教改立项 </span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt; text-indent: -66pt; margin-left: 66pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2008-2010<span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">建设集多种教学模式为一体的大学英语教学体系<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>国家级纵向项目，国家级基金项目 </span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2009-2011<span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">不断创新教学模式，有效提高学生语言综合能力<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>横向项目 </span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2009-2010<span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">培养学生创造性学习能力，提高英语综合应用能力<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>横向项目 </span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">2010-2012<span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">深化教学改革，提高教学水平<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>国家级纵向项目，国家级基金项目 </span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></div>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12pt">四、近年出版的书籍</span><span style="font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"> <br>
</span><span style="font-size: 12pt">《新题型大学英语王牌<span lang="EN-US">-</span>语法》<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>主编<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span>科学出版社<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span>2006</span>年</span></p>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt">《新时代英语听说教程》<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span>副主编<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>机械工业出版社<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>2008</span>年</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12pt">《大学英语词汇简明记忆辞典<span lang="EN-US">--</span>一般要求词汇》 主编 化学工业出版社<span lang="EN-US"> 2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12pt">《英语作文分类语汇新词典》 <span>&nbsp;</span>编者<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span>北京大学出版社<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span>2009</span>年</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: -258pt; margin-left: 258pt"><span style="font-size: 12pt">《新世代交互英语（第三版）视听说 第四册》<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">国家级精品教材<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt">编者<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>清华大学出版社<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span>2011</span>年</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12pt">《北京化工大学大学英语课程学习指导手册》 副主编 化学工业出版社<span lang="EN-US"> 2012</span>年</span></div>
