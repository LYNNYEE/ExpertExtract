<p style="widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt">彭良雪：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt">男，博士，教授，博士生导师，<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年生人，专业为基础数学，研究方向为拓扑学。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt">欢迎对拓扑学感兴趣的学生报考我的硕士与博士。主要研究方向有：广义度量空间、覆盖性质、D-空间、函数空间、序与偏序空间、拓扑群与拓扑代数等。彭良雪近年来在D-空间理论、函数空间、拓扑群等研究领域得到了多个研究成果，解决了几个公开问题。到目前彭良雪已在国内外权威或核心期刊上发表拓扑学论文<span lang="EN-US">50</span>多篇<span lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>(SCI 收录24</span>篇，<span lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>ISTP 收录1</span>篇<span lang="EN-US">)</span>。&nbsp;</span></font></font><font size="4"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt"><strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></span></font></p>
<p style="widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><font size="4"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt"><strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></strong></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt">曾于<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，作为国家公派访问学者在加拿大多伦多大学数学系留学进修一年。在<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年曾获北京市属市管高等学校中青年骨干教师称号。</span></font></p>
<p style="widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: -18pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt 48pt; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" align="left"><font size="4"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>3<span style="line-height: normal; font-variant: normal; font-style: normal; font-size: 7pt; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt">证明了紧<span lang="EN-US">-</span>似空间的可数积空间是</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">Lindel</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">öf</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt">空间（<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年）。</span></font></p>
<p style="widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt 30pt; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" align="left"><strong><span><font size="4" face="宋体">&nbsp;&nbsp;三<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span>D-</span>空间理论、函数空间及拓扑群等方面</font></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left; widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt 30pt; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" align="left">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="widows: 1; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; font: 12px Tahoma, 宋体, Arial; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(0,0,0); word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
</span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4">彭良雪在覆盖性质、<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间、对偶理论、函数空间及拓扑群等方面取得了一些有影响的结果，具体如下：</font></font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 24pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><font size="4" face="宋体">(1) 2014年得到单调正规空间是与秩不超过2的散布空间对偶，并得到一些有关树及广义树的一些结论。</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 24pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><font size="4" face="宋体">(2) 2013得到Cut^2空间的分离性等结论。</font></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 12pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp; (3) 2012</span>年，彭良雪得到两个序数的积空间是遗传离散对偶空间的结论，这回答了<span lang="EN-US">Alas</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Junqueira</span>及<span lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>Wilson</span>于<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年提出的问题。<span lang="EN-US">(</span>该文<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录<span lang="EN-US">,</span></span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; color: rgb(51,51,51); font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span><span class="frlabel1"><span style="font-weight: normal">IDS<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span></span></span><span class="frlabel1"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; color: rgb(51,51,51); font-size: 12pt">号<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; color: rgb(51,51,51); font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>855KL)</span></font></font></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (4)<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;2011</span>年，彭良雪与李慧证明的</span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">单调可数亚紧（单调亚<span lang="EN-US">Lindel</span></span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">ö</span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">f</span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">）的单调正规空间是遗传仿紧的<span lang="EN-US">,</span>这个结论解决了<span lang="EN-US">H.R. Bennett, K.P. Hart<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span>和<span lang="EN-US">D.J. Lutzer</span>提出的一个公开问题。</span></font></font></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 12pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">&nbsp; (5) 彭良雪在<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年发表的文章讨论了<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间、线性<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间与传递<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间的关系，证明了亚<span lang="EN-US">Lindel</span></span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">ö</span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US">f</span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">空间是传递<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间。<span lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>(</span>该文<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录<span lang="EN-US">, IDS: 527VQ )</span>。</span></font></font></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 12pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4">&nbsp; (6) 在<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年，彭良雪证明了有限个序数的积空间是离散对偶空间，以及两个序数积空间的正规子空间是离散对偶空间，回答了<span lang="EN-US">Alas</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Junqueira</span>及<span lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>Wilson</span>于<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年提出的问题。这两结果所在的两篇文章的<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录号分别为<span lang="EN-US">IDS: 442EH</span>与<span lang="EN-US">IDS: 508AD</span>。</font></font></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 18pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">&nbsp;(7) 于<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年，彭良雪证明了每个广义序空间是离散对偶空间，这一结果回答了<span lang="EN-US">Buzyakova R. Z.</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Tkachuk V. V.</span>及<span lang="EN-US"><span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span>Wilson R. <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (8)<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">在<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年，彭良雪年发表在<span lang="EN-US">HOUSTON JOURNAL OF MATHEMATICS</span>上的文章证明了仿紧<span lang="EN-US">C-</span>散布空间的可数积是<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间。该文被<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录（<span lang="EN-US">IDS Number: 279NC</span>）。<span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></span></font></font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 18pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">&nbsp;(9) 在<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年，彭良雪证明了由有限个<span lang="EN-US">Moore</span>子空间并的空间是<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间。该结论回了<span lang="EN-US">Arhangel</span>’<span lang="EN-US">skii</span>教授于<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年在<span lang="EN-US">Proceedings of American Mathematical Society</span>上提出的一公开问题。该文已被<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录（<span lang="EN-US">IDS Number: 181VI</span>）。</span></font></font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 12pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt">&nbsp; (10) 在<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年，彭良雪证明了如果空间<span lang="EN-US">X</span>是可数紧空间且可以表示成可数个<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间的并，则<span lang="EN-US">X</span>是<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间，因而是紧空间。该结论也回答了<span lang="EN-US">Arhangel</span>’<span lang="EN-US">skii</span>教授于<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年在<span lang="EN-US">Proceedings of American Mathematical Society</span>上提出的一公开问题。该文已被<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录（<span lang="EN-US">IDS Number: 181VI</span>）。</span></font></font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 18px; text-indent: 12pt; margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt"><font face="宋体"><font size="4">&nbsp;&nbsp; 彭良雪在<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年证明了<span lang="EN-US">C<sub>p</sub>(t<sub>omega</sub>)</span>是<span lang="EN-US">D-</span>空间。这一结果也回答了<span lang="EN-US">Buzyakova<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span>于<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年提出的一公开问题<span lang="EN-US">,</span>该文章被<span lang="EN-US">SCI</span>收录（<span lang="EN-US">IDS Number: 122TK</span>）<span lang="EN-US">;</span></font></font></span></p>
